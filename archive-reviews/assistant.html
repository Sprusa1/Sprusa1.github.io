Assistant to the Villain by Hannah Nicole Maehrer

September 25, 2023 - October 1, 2023

4/5

Star icon by Icons8

Audiobook

New Adult/Adult, Romantasy, Fantasy, Romance, Humor

Update: 15%

Thoughts so far

  • This is so absurd but I’m having so much fun with it!
  • Love Kingsley.
  • I laughed so hard when the Villain revealed his name. Fantastic snippet of a scene
  • Evie has no sense of self-preservation. Loving the sarcastic/berating inner monologue
  • This whole novel is very ridiculous/unserious/self-indulgent
  • Dialogue is very cringe but I assume that’s intentional given the whole vibes of the book

So far a very entertaining palate cleanser after my last few horror novels. It’s ridiculous, self-indulgent, and cringey at times. (The dialogue is not great but I’m taking it as a purposeful choice). It feels abundantly clear that this was made for TikTok, but I’m still entertained.

My favorite character so far is Kingsley. I’m a pun person so his introduction was a highlight.
