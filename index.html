Welcome to my book blog!

This website started as a project in my Digital Publishing course to test my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and github software. It very quickly grew from a small profile to a full blog. Here you can find all the thoughts and opinions I have about the books I've been reading. Check out my most recent reviews here on the home page or look back through the archive for older reads. Note, all of my reviews contain spoiler tags and content warnings. Happing reading!

The Inheritance Games

When a reclusive billionaire leaves her all his money, Avery becomes entangled in the riddles Tobias Hawthorne left behind--and the eyes of his grandsons.

Cemetery Boys

In order to prove himself a real brujo, Yadriel performs a summoning ceremony in secret, but he summons the wrong ghost and bad boy Julian refuses to pass on quietly.

The House in the Cerulean Sea

By-the-book case worker Linus Baker has been assigned to investigate a magical orphanage and determine if the children may bring about the end of the world.

Red, White & Royal Blue

After causing an international PR nightmare, First Son Alex is forced to play nice with Prince Henry of England, but their blossoming relationship could cause problems for both of their countries.

Under the Whispering Door

When Wallace Price encounters Hugo the Ferryman, he realizes he isn't ready to pass on to whatever comes next, but to truly live and enjoy life.

The Sunbearer Trials

As a Jade semidiose, not much is expected of Teo. But when he is chosen for the Sunbearer Trials, he must ensure that he and his friends don't become the next sacrifice.

Katzenjammer

Cat and her classmates have been trapped in School and changed into something else. As more and more students are found in pieces, Cat will have to face the truth of her past in order to save herself from the same fate.

Lost in the Never Woods

Wendy Darling is still reeling from her brothers' five year disappearnce and the memories she can't recall. When more kids disappear, a mysterious boy claims she is the only one who can find them.

The Girl Who Fell Beneath the Sea

To appease the Sea God's wrath and save her brother's beloved, Mina tosses herself into the sea. But when she discovers the god under a cursed sleep, it is up to Mina to break the curse and end her people's suffering.

Together We Rot

Wil teams up with her ex-friend Elwood to uncover the secrets behind his family's church: The Garden of Adam. But the Morguewood grows hungry, and Elwood may be their next sacrifice.

Hide

Contestants compete in a Hide & Seek contest at an abandoned amusement park. But as the days progress, they start to question who or what is hunting them and have contestants been eliminated permanently?

Hallowe'en Party

Regarded as one of her darkest mysteries, this Agatha Christie novel follows Hercule Poirot as he investigates the murder of a young girl drowned in a bucket of apples.

The Sacrifice

A Hollywood crew arrives on the cursed island of Kisapmata to uncover the secrets of the Dreamer god. Local teen Alon knows if they can't convince the crew to leave, they will be the island's next victims.

Assisstant to the Villain

Evie accidentally runs into The Villain and becomes his personal assistant. When they discover evidence of a mole, Evie must root out the traitor to save her new family and the boss she can't help but love.

In the Hall with the Knife

In this YA retelling of the popular board game Clue, students are snowed in at Blackbrook Academy and discover the body of their headmaster, murdered in the night.
