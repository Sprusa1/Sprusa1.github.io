The Sunbearer Trials

Percy Jackson meets The Hunger Games, in this Mexican-influenced fantasy. Teo is a Jade semidiose son of Quetzal, the semidiosa of birds. As a Jade he will never amount to much beyond a public figure within his home city of Quetzlan, not like the Golds who become heroes renowned around... But this year is the Sunbearer Trials and for the first time two Jades have been chosen to compete. Navigating a world he was never meant to be part of and a competition he was not prepared, Teo must keep his friends alive because while the winner is honored as Sunbearer, the loser is sacrificed to fuel Sol's protection for another decade.

Katzenjammer

In this dark horror novel, Cat is desperately searching for a way to escape School. For as long as she can remember, she and her classmates have been trapped inside the living, breathing entity that is School. No one enters, no one leaves, and those unfortuante enough to be outcast have started changing. As more and more transformed are found in pieces, Cat will have to face the truth of her past in order to save herself from the same fate.

Lost in the Never Woods

A modern retelling of Peter Pan, Aiden Thomas' sophomore novel centers on a Wendy Darling still reeling from her brothers' disappearnce five years ago and the memories she can't recall. When more kids go missing, a mysterious boy claims she is the only one with the power to find them.

The Girl Who Fell Beneath the Sea

An ode to (look up original folktale), Mina takes the place of the Sea God's Bride to appease his wrath and save her brother's true love. But upon entering the spirit world, she discoves the Sea God under a cursed sleep, leaving the ocean to ravage her country in his absence. It is up to Mina and her new friends to break the curse and end her people's suffering.

Together We Rot

In Skyla Arndt's debut paranormal romance, Wil teams up with her ex-best friend Elwood to investigate her mother's disappearance and uncover the secrets behind his family's church: The Garden of Adam. But the Morguewood grows hungry, and Elwood may be their next sacrifice.

Hide

A supernatural thriller, this Kiersten White novel follows the unwitting contestants of a Hide and Seek contest within an abandoned amusement park. But as more people are found, they start to question who or what is hunting them and have contestants been eliminated permanently?

Hallowe'en Party

Regarded as one of her darkest mysteries, this Agatha Christie novel follows Hercule Poirot as he investigates the murder of young Joyce Reynolds, who was drowned in a bucket of apples. This novel is the inspiration for A Haunting in Venice.
